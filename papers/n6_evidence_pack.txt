1. The single identity
----------------------

Everything in this stack is a verification of one equation:

    sigma(n) * phi(n)  =  n * tau(n)       uniquely for   n = 6   (n >= 2)

For n = 6:   sigma=12, phi=2, tau=4   →   12 * 2 = 24 = 6 * 4   ✓

Test it yourself on any other small n and it fails.
